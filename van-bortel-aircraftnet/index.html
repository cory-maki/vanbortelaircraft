Our Story

Four Decades of Trusted Cessna Sales

Howard G. Van Bortel founded Van Bortel Aircraft in 1985 with $1,000 and a card table in his sister's living room. After learning to fly a Cessna 150 on a grass strip in Upstate New York a decade earlier, he transformed his passion for aviation into a company built on integrity, trust, and exceptional customer service.

Today, Van Bortel Aircraft is a privately owned company with approximately 50 employees operating from Arlington Municipal Airport in Texas. The company is designated as a Cessna Propjet Service Center—one of only ten in the United States—and its dedicated financing division has helped customers finance Cessna ownership for more than a decade.

The company continues to operate according to the three principles established by Howard G. Van Bortel at its founding: care for people, build trust through integrity, and maintain a long-term perspective. These values remain the foundation of every customer relationship and business decision. To learn more about the company's history and values, read the full Van Bortel Aircraft story.
