Our Story

Four Decades of Trusted Cessna Sales

Howard G. Van Bortel founded Van Bortel Aircraft in 1985 with $1,000 in the bank and a card table in his sister's living room. He had learned to fly a decade earlier in a Cessna 150 on a grass strip in Upstate New York, and that early passion for aviation became the foundation of a company built on trust, principle, and genuine care for customers.

Today Van Bortel Aircraft is a privately owned company of 50 employees operating out of Arlington Municipal Airport in Texas. The company holds a Cessna Propjet Service Center designation, one of only ten in the United States, and its dedicated financing arm has helped customers fund Cessna ownership for well over a decade.

Every sale still runs through the same three principles Howard set on day one: care about people, trust people, and think long term. Read the full Van Bortel Aircraft story to see how those principles built the world's most trusted Cessna dealer.
